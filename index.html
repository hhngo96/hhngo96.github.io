Welcome

I am a first-year PhD student in Transportation Engineering at the University of Memphis, advised by Prof. Sabya Mishra. My main area of research interest is Data-driven Approaches and Applications for Urban Planning, This includes econometric methods for transportation (discrete choice modeling), optimization models, modified neural network framework for graph structure data.

I completed my bachelor degree in 2018 at the University of Memphis where I received the Dr. Tzu Senior Design award and won third place in the Seismic Design Competition. My involvement in scientific research began in the junior year and I published my first paper in the Transportation Research Part - A journal a year after.
